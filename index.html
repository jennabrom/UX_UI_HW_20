User Needs & Goals:
  • Find an affordable, yet desirable accommodations
  • Getaway from busy lifestyle in the city
  • Needs to stay within budget, for numerous trips

User Pain Points & Frustrations:
  • Unable to find reliable reviews
  • Unable to filter results for their needs
  • Struggling to figure out which part of trip to start booking

Data Gathering Process

I began conducting 5 interviews and 4 surveys from frequent and infrequent travelers ranging from ages 24-35, who on average traveled 2-20 times per year.

Definition

Problem Statement:

We have observed that the users, are frustrated with needed to find affordable, flexible accommodations that align with their busy lifestyles, when causes them to have less quality and frequent travel experiences.

User Insight:

Alice Simmons, a single working individual, needs to find an affordable, flexible travel accommodations because she desires to get away and find new experiences since she is a busy, stressed out nurse.

Ideation

Feature Prioritization

Key Findings
  • Centralized booking app for lodging, transportation and experiences
  • Want to create trip around personalized budget
  • Make changes to trip without fees

Value Proposition Statement

My organization is developing a way for travelers to book an entire trip based on their budget and allow travelers the flexibility to make their own changes as needed.
